Key Findings
Ensemble Superiority
Stacked ensemble gains +1.1pp R² over XGBoost alone by combining temporal (LSTM), boosting (XGB, LGB) and meta-learner signals.
XGBoost Tuning +5.5%
Grid search over 64 trials improved R² from 0.872 (default) to 0.920. learning_rate was most sensitive (28%).
LSTM Convergence
256 units with 30% dropout prevented overfitting. Val loss stabilised at epoch 35. Seq length 3 optimal for 5-season data.
Sentiment Signal 19.2%
VADER compound + social buzz score contribute 19.2% of XGBoost feature gain — confirming sentiment predicts transfer value shifts.
